About Me

My name is Martin Engen, and I’m from Østfold in Norway. I am very interested in computers and technology, and thus I have chosen an education and a career within this wide world of computing.

It all began back in high school where I attended a class going through a 2-week course of basic HTML, this intrigued me and I began exploring how internet browsers worked. This lead me to Flash, and through YouTube tutorials I developed my first browser game, a 2d platformer based on Super Mario.

I have since then gotten a bachelor’s degree within computer technology, and through my studies I have been into some cool areas of computer science: full-stack web development, algorithms, databases, cloud computing and misc. general software development.
